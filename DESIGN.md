# Reelio Video Identity

## Style Prompt

Warm, premium real-estate SaaS with a practical workspace feel: ivory surfaces, navy typography, charcoal depth, and restrained burnt-orange/gold action accents. The video should feel cinematic enough for property media but organized like a professional tool, with UI panels, image frames, status tags, and smooth editorial transitions rather than decorative spectacle.

## Colors

- `#f8f7f3` — warm ivory canvas
- `#ffffff` — raised UI surface
- `#182238` — primary navy text
- `#41506b` — muted slate labels
- `#df6f00` — Reelio action gold
- `#f18700` — bright gold highlight
- `#202126` — charcoal shell/depth

## Typography

- Primary UI and display: `Heebo`, falling back to `ui-sans-serif`
- Numeric/data labels: same family with `font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums`
- Wordmark treatment: lowercase, generous spacing restraint, no decorative serif

## Motion

- Smooth editorial pacing with blur crossfades, warm light leaks, and restrained push transitions.
- Entrances should feel like product UI assembling into place, not bouncing or spinning.
- Ambient motion can use slow image drift, gold line sweeps, and gentle panel parallax.

## What NOT to Do

- No purple/blue SaaS gradients or neon tech palette.
- No black-box cyber dashboards; Reelio is warm, clear, and property-focused.
- No generic stock-photo hero cards without product UI context.
- No oversized marketing page mockups that hide the actual workflow.
- No jump cuts between scenes.
